E-Commerce Revenue Analysis - Key Business Insights


* Technology category generated the highest sales revenue.
* Office Supplies category generated better profit margins despite lower sales.
* Furniture category underperformed in profitability.
* Tables sub-category generated the highest losses.
* Copiers sub-category generated the highest profit.
* High discounts negatively impacted profitability.
* Standard Class shipping had the highest number of orders and sales contribution.
* West region generated the highest sales and profit.
* Central region showed comparatively weaker performance.
* Some high-sales customers generated low or negative profit.
* Overall business profit margin was around 12.47%.
* Sales and profit showed overall growth from 2014 to 2017.

Recommendations-

* Reduce excessive discounting on low-profit products to improve profitability.
* Review pricing and product strategy for the Furniture category, especially Tables.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-profit categories such as Technology and Copiers.
* Analyze the causes of weak performance in the Central region and implement targeted improvement strategies.
* Identify high-sales customers generating low profits and optimize discount policies for those accounts.
* Continue strengthening Standard Class shipping operations due to its high contribution to sales.
* Expand successful sales strategies from the West region to other regions where feasible.
* Regularly monitor category-level profitability to identify loss-making products early.
